What services do you offer?

We provide real-time data on blood availability, organ donations, and hospital facilities.

How can I register my hospital?

You can register by filling out the form on the Contact Us page or reaching out via email.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we ensure that all data is encrypted and protected with the highest security standards.

How do I find blood or organ donors quickly?

You can use our search feature to locate donors or availability in real time.

Are there any membership fees?

No, our platform is completely free for both hospitals and patients.

Can I track my blood donation history?

Yes, registered users can view their donation history and eligibility for the next donation.

How frequently is the data updated?

Data is updated every few minutes to provide the most accurate and reliable information.
